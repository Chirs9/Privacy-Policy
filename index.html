Privacy Policy for FinaGo

Last Updated: 11/3/2025

Introduction

FinaGo is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application FinaGo. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Financial Data: Transaction records, asset information, debt details, budget categories, and financial goals that you manually enter into the app.
  • Photos: Receipt images when you use our AI-powered receipt scanner feature.
  • Group Information: When using Spleet feature, we collect group names, member information, and shared expense data.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, crash reports, and performance analytics.
  • Location Data: We do not collect precise location data, but may derive general location from IP address for currency localization.

Information from Third Parties

  • Advertising Partners: Our advertising partners may collect information about your device and usage patterns to serve relevant ads.
  • AI Services: When using our educational AI assistant, your questions are processed by third-party AI services.

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Process and store your financial data locally on your device.
  • Improve receipt scanning accuracy and AI assistant responses.
  • Enable Spleet functionality for group expense management.
  • Monitor progress toward your financial objectives.

Analytics and Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Understand which features are most valuable to users.
  • Identify and resolve technical issues.

Advertising

  • Display relevant advertisements based on general usage patterns.
  • Measure ad effectiveness and optimize ad placement.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • All financial data is stored locally on your device using secure Android storage mechanisms.
  • Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption methods.
  • We do not automatically upload your data to cloud servers.

Data Security Measures

  • Data protection relies on your device's built-in security features.
  • We implement secure coding practices and regular security updates.
  • Only you have access to your data through the app.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial information to third parties.

Limited Sharing Scenarios

  • Anonymous usage data for ad personalization (Google AdMob, Meta Audience Network).
  • AI questions processed by third-party services (not stored).
  • Legal compliance if required by law or court order.
  • Business transfers in case of merger or acquisition (with notice).

Spleet Data Sharing

  • Shared expense info is visible to group members.
  • You consent to this when joining or creating groups.

Third-Party Services

Advertising Networks

  • Google AdMob: Serves ads, collects device and usage data.
  • Meta Audience Network: Provides additional ad inventory.
  • Privacy Policies:
    • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
    • Meta Privacy Policy

AI Services

AI queries are processed by third-party providers but not stored.

Analytics Services

Firebase Analytics and Google Play Services collect anonymous statistics and crash data.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access, export, or delete your data directly in the app.
  • Disable ad personalization in device settings.
  • Opt out of ad tracking through privacy settings.
  • Control app notifications and permissions.

Children's Privacy

FinaGo is not intended for children under 13 years old. If a child has provided information, contact us to remove it.

International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in other countries with appropriate safeguards.

Data Retention

  • Financial data remains on your device until deleted or uninstalled.
  • Anonymous usage data retained for up to 26 months.
  • Crash reports retained up to 12 months.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
